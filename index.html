About Me

I began my career as an engineer at Vanderbilt University. I graduated with a Bachelor's of Science in Engineering Science. Through my course work, I had to take 2 coding classes (Intro to Java and Matlab), but didn't really gain a passion for it until I was hired into my first position. In that position, I did a lot of coding to transform text files and learned I had a knact and passion for coding. From there I pursued some peersonal study until I joined the GT Coding Bootcamp where I learned the skills necessary to be a Full Stack Developer. A few fun facts about myself: I am a diehard Pittsburgh sports fan. Whether it's the Steelers, Penguins, or Pirates, I am always watching a game. I spent 2 years as a walk on for the Vanderbilt football team. As such, I spend most of my fall lamenting the woes of our team.
